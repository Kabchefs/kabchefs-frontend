About Kabchefs

Kabchefs is an open source and non-profitable organization. Lots of #startups failed with a lack of technical team support. They face a lack of man-power , money , assets, liquidity,and other resources. They lose the race in their Pre-launching stage. We give #FREE technical support to these early-stage startups.In free time, We develop an #Indian version of various software,websites,web apps,android apps,and ios #apps to contribute to the "vocal for local" initiative.
Our team is the best in their technical skills. They are active with various open source communities. They have worked with almost all stack like-MEAN , MERN , LAMP , MVC, Kotlin-Android,React-Native,Swift,Laravel,Symfony Php, Codelgniter,Django,ML and WordPress(themes+plugin),etc.
We also help interns and students to enhance their open-source development skills and technical skills. Our mentors are from IIT's,NIT'S and various prestigious institutes. The individual guidance from mentors makes #interns, student's technical skills strong.
